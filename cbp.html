Felix Lego City International Airport is home to a full, independent Republic States Customs and Border Protection processing facility — the only on-airport CBP site in Lego City. All international passengers arriving at FLX are processed through this facility before entering the Republic States.

International Arrivals Process
Step-by-Step: Arriving at FLX
1
Deplane & Follow Signage
Upon landing, deplane and follow the green "International Arrivals / Customs & Border Protection" signs throughout Terminal 1. All arrivals are directed to Level 1 of the Arrivals Hall via dedicated corridors from every gate. Do not follow signs for Departures or Connections at this stage.
💡 Stay with your group. Transfer passengers connecting to a domestic flight must still clear CBP before re-entering the secure area.
2
Collect Your Baggage
Proceed to Baggage Claim Carousels 1–8 on the Arrivals Level. Check the overhead screens for your flight number and assigned carousel. Collect all checked bags before proceeding to CBP — you must present all luggage during inspection.
💡 Keep your baggage claim tickets. They may be checked against your bags by CBP officers.
3
Complete Your Customs Declaration Form
All arriving passengers must complete a RS Customs Declaration Form (Form RS-CBP-6059). Paper forms are available throughout the arrival corridors and at baggage claim. One form per family or travel group is accepted. You must declare all items including gifts, food, currency over $10,000 RS, and commercial merchandise.
⚠ Failure to declare required items may result in fines, seizure of goods, or denial of entry. When in doubt — declare it.
4
Proceed to CBP Processing — Level 1, Arrivals Hall
Enter the CBP processing hall and join the appropriate queue based on your travel document. Lane signage is clearly posted at the hall entrance. Republic States citizens and permanent residents use the RS Citizens lane. All other passport holders use the All Passports lanes.
💡 Have your passport, completed declaration form, and any supporting documents (visa, travel authorization, return ticket) ready before approaching the primary inspection booth.
5
Primary Inspection
A CBP Officer will review your travel documents, ask questions about your trip, and review your declaration form. Biometric data (fingerprints and photograph) will be collected from eligible non-RS-citizen travelers. Answer all questions honestly and completely. This process typically takes 1–3 minutes per traveler.
⚠ Electronic devices may be inspected or detained by CBP officers at their discretion without a warrant.
6
Secondary Inspection (If Referred)
Some travelers may be referred to secondary inspection by the primary CBP Officer. This is routine and does not necessarily indicate wrongdoing. In secondary inspection, officers may conduct a more thorough review of documents, baggage, and declarations. Agricultural items, currency, and commercial goods receive special attention here.
💡 Secondary inspection is conducted in a secure area off the main hall. Follow officer instructions and remain calm — the process is typically completed within 20–40 minutes.
7
Agriculture & Customs Screening
After primary inspection, all bags pass through the RS Agricultural Screening Area before you exit. Declared agricultural items will be inspected by Agriculture specialists. Prohibited items will be confiscated. You may be asked to open bags for physical inspection — cooperate fully with Agriculture officers.
⚠ Agricultural violations — even unintentional ones — carry significant fines. All food, plant, soil, and animal products must be declared.
8
Exit to the Arrivals Hall
Once cleared by CBP and Agriculture, proceed through the exit doors into the public Arrivals Hall on Level 1. Ground transportation, taxis, rideshare, and the FLX Metro are all accessible from Level 1. Welcome to Lego City and the Republic States.
💡 Connecting passengers: re-check any bags at the Domestic Baggage Re-Check counter (Level 1, near the T2 connector) and proceed to Terminal 2 via the airport connector.

Trusted Traveler Program
Global Entry Available at FLX
FLX CBP offers Global Entry for pre-approved trusted travelers, providing expedited clearance upon arrival. Global Entry members bypass the standard primary inspection queue and use dedicated automated kiosks. However, Global Entry kiosks are not self-service at FLX — please approach any CBP Officer upon arrival and inform them you are a Global Entry member. They will redirect you to the appropriate kiosk or processing area.
🟢 Global Entry is available during all staffed hours. Members must still complete a customs declaration if carrying declarable items.
On-Site Facilities
Full Inspection Complex
🔍
Primary Inspection Hall
Full Facility

A dedicated primary inspection hall with 4 staffed booths — 3 All Passports lanes and 1 RS Citizens lane. All international arriving passengers are processed here before entering the Republic States.

Equipped with biometric fingerprint and facial recognition systems, document scanners, and secure officer workstations. All booths are fully accessible.

🗂
Secondary Inspection Suite
On-Site

A secure secondary inspection area off the main processing hall, used for more detailed review of travelers, documents, and baggage referred by primary inspection officers.

Includes private interview rooms, full baggage X-ray screening, and secure holding areas. Legal counsel information is posted in the secondary suite.

🌿
Agriculture Inspection
Full Facility

A full RS Department of Agriculture inspection area staffed by Agriculture Specialists. All international baggage passes through this area after primary CBP inspection.

Equipped with X-ray screening, detector dog teams, and physical inspection tables. Prohibited agricultural items are confiscated and may be incinerated on-site.

💼
Customs Examination
Full Facility

Dedicated customs examination area for detailed inspection of declared goods, commercial merchandise, currency, and high-value items. Duty assessment and collection is handled here.

Currency declarations over $10,000 RS must be reported and may be verified. Commercial goods importers should have all invoices and documentation ready.

📦
Seized & Detained Property
On-Site

Secure storage facility for items detained or seized by CBP or Agriculture officers. Travelers who have items detained will receive a written receipt detailing the item and grounds for detention.

Appeals and inquiries regarding seized property can be initiated at the CBP Administrative Office (Level 1, east corridor).

🏥
Medical & Public Health
On-Site

RS Public Health Service Officers are stationed at FLX CBP to respond to travelers exhibiting signs of illness or arriving from areas with active public health alerts.

Medical assessment rooms are adjacent to the secondary inspection suite. Travelers with medical concerns should notify a CBP Officer immediately upon arrival.

Restricted & Prohibited Items
What Not to Bring into the Republic States
⚠️
This is not an exhaustive list. RS CBP regulations are detailed and subject to change. When in doubt, declare the item on your customs form — officers will advise you. Failure to declare prohibited or restricted items may result in fines up to $10,000 RS, criminal prosecution, or denial of entry.
🌿
Agricultural & Food Items
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables (unless from approved sources)
  • Soil, dirt, or mud on footwear or equipment
  • Live plants, cuttings, seeds, or bulbs without a phytosanitary certificate
  • Unprocessed animal products: meat, poultry, eggs, dairy (from most countries)
  • Live insects, snails, or other invertebrates
  • Homemade or commercially prepared foods from high-risk countries
  • Cut flowers and foliage without inspection certificate
🔫
Weapons & Dangerous Items
  • Firearms without prior RS import authorization and declaration
  • Ammunition without proper import license
  • Switchblades, butterfly knives, and concealed blades
  • Explosives, fireworks, or flammable materials
  • Stun guns and tasers without RS permit
  • Replica or imitation firearms (subject to inspection)
  • Chemical agents or toxic substances
💊
Drugs & Controlled Substances
  • Narcotics and controlled substances without RS prescription
  • Prescription medications exceeding 90-day personal supply
  • Medications not in original labeled containers
  • Supplements containing banned substances
  • Any substance on the RS Controlled Substances Schedule I–V
  • Drug paraphernalia of any kind
🐾
Animals & Wildlife
  • Live animals without RS veterinary import certificate and health certificate
  • Endangered species or products thereof (CITES-listed)
  • Ivory, rhino horn, coral, or tortoiseshell products
  • Feathers, skins, or bones of protected bird and animal species
  • Exotic pets without prior RS Fish & Wildlife Service approval
  • Animal by-products from high-risk disease regions
💵
Currency & Financial Instruments
  • Cash or monetary instruments over $10,000 RS without declaration (RS Form FinCen 105)
  • Counterfeit currency of any denomination
  • Undeclared traveler's checks or money orders over $10,000 RS
  • Proceeds of criminal activity in any form
  • Counterfeit coins, stamps, or government-issued instruments
📀
Intellectual Property & Other
  • Counterfeit goods, pirated media, or fake branded merchandise
  • Obscene or pornographic materials (subject to RS law)
  • Seditious or treasonous materials against the Republic States
  • Items from RS-embargoed countries without OFAC license
  • Lottery tickets or gambling devices from prohibited jurisdictions
  • Hazardous waste or radioactive materials
