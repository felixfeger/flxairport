International Arrivals · Terminal 1
Republic States Customs & Border Protection
Terminal 1 hosts a full, independent RS Customs and Border Protection facility — the only on-airport CBP site in Lego City. All international passengers are processed here with dedicated primary screening, secondary inspection, Global Entry kiosks, and specialized agricultural and currency screening areas.
