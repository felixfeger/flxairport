How It Works
FLX Shuttle Service Guide
🚌
The Fleet
Brand new MCI Coach buses operated by City Metro on a fully dedicated busway. Buses are climate-controlled with luggage storage, accessibility ramps, and digital displays.
  • 3–5 buses active at all times
  • Maximum 2 buses per stop at once
  • Route: Lot → T1 → T2 → Lot (loop)
  • Every 2–5 min standard; 90 sec rush hour
📍
Three Stops
The FLX Shuttle makes exactly three stops on its continuous loop route:
  • FLX Consolidated Lot — Main parking hub, covered waiting area, luggage carts
  • Terminal 1 — International arrivals & departures level
  • Terminal 2 — Domestic departures & arrivals level
⏱
Timing & Tips
Allow extra time when using the shuttle, especially during peak travel hours.
  • Lot to T1: approx. 6–8 minutes
  • T1 to T2: approx. 4 minutes via connector road
  • T2 back to Lot: approx. 6 minutes
  • Arrive at lot at least 90 min before departure
