City Metro FLX Shuttle
How the Shuttle Works
🚌
The Fleet
Brand-new MCI Coach buses operated by City Metro on a fully dedicated busway — no traffic, no delays.
  • 3–5 buses active at all times
  • Maximum 2 buses per stop at once
  • Climate-controlled with luggage storage
  • Full ADA accessibility ramps
📍
Three Stops
Continuous loop — three stops, no detours:
  • FLX Consolidated Lot — covered waiting area, luggage carts
  • Terminal 1 — International departures & arrivals level
  • Terminal 2 — Domestic departures & arrivals level
⏱
Journey Times
Dedicated busway means consistent journey times every time:
  • Lot → Terminal 1: ~6–8 minutes
  • Terminal 1 → Terminal 2: ~4 minutes
  • Terminal 2 → Lot: ~6 minutes
  • Arrive at lot 90+ min before departure
